Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a great with honey. They are relatively simple to make and even moreso if you already have premade batter.

They are also time saving and can be served with syrup or honey.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons ofvegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Pour in the egg, oil and milk. Then continue mixing until smooth and there are no lumps.
  3. Lightly oil a frying pan or griddle over medium high heat.
  4. Pour approximately 1/4 batter onto the pan or griddle.
  5. Wait until bubbles start to form and flip. Make sure the pancakes are brown on both sides and serve.
