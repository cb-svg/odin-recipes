Sandwich

Description

This is a ham sandwich. It is a simple meal that is fast and easy to make. It can be eaten at any time of day.

This is a really good meal since it is time saving and cooking the ham is optional.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Spread mayonnaise on two slices of bread(optional: toast bread)
  2. Place lettuce on one of the slices
  3. Place the cheese
  4. Finish with the ham(optional: cook ham) and the other slice of bread
